The TURN service is a LoadBalancer — it may take a few minutes for an external IP to be assigned.
Watch its status with:

  kubectl get svc --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} {{ include "galene.fullname" . }}-turn -w

Once the IP is available, the init container will write it to the pod's .env file automatically on next start.
